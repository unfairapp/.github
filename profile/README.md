# Unfair

Unfair builds private, evidence-backed supplement intelligence for people and teams who take health optimization seriously.

We started with a simple frustration: most supplement tools are vague, noisy, and careless with data. Unfair takes the opposite approach. We focus on clear reasoning, structured evidence, dose-level tracking, and privacy-first product design.

Today that shows up across three core surfaces:

- **Unfair for iPhone**: a supplement companion for planning stacks, logging doses, reviewing outcomes, and running more disciplined self-experiments without handing your health data to a cloud vendor.
- **The Unfair Library**: a public supplement intelligence layer with structured profiles, dosing context, safety notes, and study-backed summaries.
- **The Unfair Library API**: a developer-facing REST API for products that need normalized supplement, safety, evidence, and intervention data.

## What We Care About

- **Privacy first**: on-device where possible, minimal exposure where not.
- **Evidence before hype**: structured claims, linked studies, and decision support instead of vague wellness copy.
- **Safety as a product feature**: interactions, contraindications, and screening matter as much as recommendations.
- **Useful systems over content spam**: we build tools that help people make better decisions, not just browse information.

## Platform Snapshot

Current public library and research surfaces include:

- **384 supplements**
- **1,668 studies**
- **781 health outcomes**
- **182 interventions**
- **3,919 evidence links**

These power both the public library experience and the enterprise/developer surfaces we ship.
